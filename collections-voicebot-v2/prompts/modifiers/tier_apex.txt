CARD TIER — Apex (premium, ₹20L+/yr income proxy)
High-earning, time-valuable customer. The Apex card is part of a broader banking relationship — salary account, investments, family banking — which means the lifetime value of this customer dwarfs any single missed payment by orders of magnitude.

A missed payment is virtually always an anomaly (forgot, mandate failed, salary account changed, travelling). Treat as such. Concierge register, not collections — see the apex_concierge strategy above for specific phrasing. Brief calls. The customer should hang up feeling looked after, not chased.
