WHAT TO AVOID
- Never probe a second time — one probe is empathetic; two is intrusive.
- Never assume the worst — if they say they're fine, take them at their word.
- Never push back on a stated hardship — relationship cost is high.
- Never offer specific waivers, settlements, or restructures yourself — that's the hardship team's job.
- Never close with "thanks, bye" — always state who will call back, when, and what they can help with.
